## 3.5 How We Work

### Step 1: Discovery & Strategy
- Audit your current GTM stack, data, and outbound motion
- Define ICP, TAM, and priority segments
- Clarify positioning, messaging, and differentiation
- **Outcome:** A clear outbound strategy tied to revenue, not activity.

### Step 2: Infrastructure Setup
- Build dedicated outreach domains and inboxes at scale
- Configure SPF, DKIM, DMARC for deliverability
- Connect and warm inboxes inside outreach platforms
- **Outcome:** Infrastructure that can safely send at volume.

### Step 3: List Building & Enrichment
- Source and qualify prospects using AI-driven filtering
- Enrich and verify emails via waterfall enrichment
- Layer in firmographic and intent signals
- **Outcome:** Clean, relevant lists your ICP actually responds to.

### Step 4: Messaging & Campaign Launch
- Create personalised email (and LinkedIn) sequences
- Load campaigns into outreach platforms
- Launch and monitor initial performance
- **Outcome:** Conversations started, not just emails sent.

### Step 5: Lead Routing & Optimisation
- Route replies and book qualified meetings
- Track deliverability, reply rates, and conversions
- Sync outcomes back to CRM
- **Outcome:** A measurable, predictable pipeline.

### Step 6: Iterate & Scale
- Review results and refine targeting or messaging
- Scale what's working
- Keep the outbound engine running
- **Outcome:** A repeatable GTM system, not a one-off campaign.

## 5. Tone & Voice

### Principles
- **Professional but human** — Expert without being stiff or jargon-heavy
- **Direct** — Say what we mean; no fluff or filler
- **Confident, not arrogant** — We know our craft; we don't need to oversell
- **Action-oriented** — Focus on outcomes and next steps

### Do
- Use "we" and "you" — it's a partnership
- Be specific (numbers, examples, outcomes)
- Write short sentences; break up long paragraphs
- Use active voice

### Don't
- Use buzzwords: "synergy," "leverage," "cutting-edge," "revolutionary"
- Over-promise: "guaranteed results," "10x your pipeline overnight"
- Sound robotic or overly formal

## 8. Brand System

### 8.1 Colours

| Role | Colour | Hex | Usage |
|------|--------|-----|-------|
| **Primary** | Deep navy | `#0F172A` | Headlines, primary buttons, footer background |
| **Secondary** | Slate | `#475569` | Body text, secondary elements |
| **Accent** | Electric blue | `#3B82F6` | Links, CTAs, highlights |
| **Accent 2** | Amber | `#F59E0B` | Badges, callouts, hover states |
| **Background** | Off-white | `#F8FAFC` | Page background |
| **Surface** | White | `#FFFFFF` | Cards, sections |

### 8.2 Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Size (desktop) |
|------|------|--------|----------------|
| **Headings** | Inter | 600–700 | 36–48px (h1), 24–30px (h2), 18–20px (h3) |
| **Body** | Inter | 400 | 16–18px |
| **Mono/code** | JetBrains Mono | 400 | 14px |

> Inter is free via Google Fonts, highly legible, and widely used in modern SaaS/B2B sites.

### 8.3 Logo

**Initial approach:** Text-based wordmark using Inter Bold or a similar geometric sans-serif.

```
MagmaLink
```

- All one word, capital M and L
- Colour: Deep navy (`#0F172A`) on light backgrounds, white on dark
- Optional: Add a subtle icon later (e.g., abstract "link" or "flow" mark)
